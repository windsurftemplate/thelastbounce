## Visual Design Specifications

### Ball Appearance
- **Base Color**: Deep charcoal gray or navy blue (#2C3E50)
- **Material**: Premium rubber with matte finish
- **Size**: Standard racquetball size (2.25" / 57mm diameter)
- **Weight**: Solid, satisfying bounce

### Engraving Specifications
- **Method**: Laser engraved or debossed (recessed)
- **Side 1 - Motivational Message**:
  - Text: "YOU ONLY LOSE / WHEN YOU STOP / BOUNCING"
  - Font: Bold, clean sans-serif (Helvetica Bold or similar)
  - Size: 5-6mm letter height
  - Color: White or gold fill for contrast

- **Side 2 - Brand Logo**:
  - Text: "THE LAST / BOUNCE"
  - Font: Same as message side
  - Size: 6-7mm letter height
  - Optional: Small icon (bouncing ball graphic)

### Premium Version Options
- **Gold edition**: Black ball with gold engraving
- **Silver edition**: Navy ball with silver engraving
- **Custom edition**: Personalized message on one side

## High-Resolution Image Mockup Requirements

Product photography style:
1. **Minimalist aesthetic**: Clean, simple background (white or gradient)

   - Hero shot: Ball centered, message visible
   - Detail shot: Close-up of engraved text

4. **Packaging**: Premium gift box (black or navy, magnetic closure)
