## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Key Risks

**1. Product Performance Risk**
- **Risk**: Balls don't maintain bounce as promised
- **Mitigation**: Extensive R&D testing, third-party ITF certification, pilot batch to 100 beta testers
- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: Low (tested for 18 months)

**2. Customer Skepticism**
- **Risk**: "Lifetime guarantee" sounds too good to be true
- **Mitigation**: 30-day money-back guarantee, video testimonials, transparent warranty terms, influencer validation
- **Impact**: Medium
- **Probability**: Medium

**3. Competitive Response**
- **Risk**: Major brands (Wilson, Penn) launch competing products
- **Mitigation**: Patent protection, first-mover advantage, build brand loyalty quickly, differentiate on mission/values
- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: Medium (18-24 month lag)

**4. Manufacturing Issues**
- **Risk**: Quality control, supply chain disruptions
- **Mitigation**: Multiple supplier quotes, quality inspections, inventory buffer, backup manufacturer relationship
- **Impact**: High
- **Probability**: Low

**5. High Return Rate**
- **Risk**: 30-day guarantee leads to excessive returns
- **Mitigation**: Clear product education, video demonstrations, strong customer support, quality product
- **Impact**: Medium
- **Probability**: Low (typical DTC: 3-5%)

**6. Marketing Channel Saturation**
- **Risk**: Facebook ads become too expensive, CAC increases
- **Mitigation**: Diversify channels early, build organic/referral engine, create content moat, email list owned asset
- **Impact**: Medium
- **Probability**: Medium

**7. Regulatory/Certification**
- **Risk**: ITF changes standards, additional regulations
- **Mitigation**: Maintain ITF relationships, stay ahead of standard changes, legal counsel on retainer
- **Impact**: Low
- **Probability**: Very Low
