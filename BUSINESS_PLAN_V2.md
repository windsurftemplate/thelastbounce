- Perceived value: $20-25 (premium packaging elevates)

**Why $12.99 Works**
- Low enough: Impulse purchase, low barrier gift
- High enough: Signals quality, not cheap tchotchke

**Premium Tiers (Future)**
- Gold edition (black ball, gold engraving): $19.99
- Custom engraving: $17.99
- Gift set (ball + journal): $29.99

### Unit Economics

**Per Unit (D2C)**
- Selling price: $12.99
- COGS: $3.90 (ball + engraving + packaging)
- Shipping: $4.00
- Payment processing (3%): $0.39
- **Gross profit per unit**: $4.70 (36%)

**After marketing (target CAC: $8)**

- **Net profit per first purchase**: -$3.30

**But here's the magic...**

- 15% become repeat buyers within 12 months
- **Lifetime value**: $18-22
- **LTV:CAC ratio**: 2.25:1 (profitable)

## Operations

### Supply Chain

**Manufacturing**
- **Supplier**: Alibaba-sourced rubber ball manufacturer (China)
- **Customization**: Laser engraving (US-based partner for speed)
- **MOQ**: 500 units initial, then 2,000 unit reorders
- **Lead time**:
  - Balls: 30 days (China)
  - Engraving: 3-5 days (US)
  - Total: 35-40 days

**Cost Breakdown (Per Unit)**
- Blank rubber ball: $1.20
- Laser engraving: $1.50
- Premium gift box: $0.80
- Philosophy card: $0.20
- Poly bag/protection: $0.20
- **Total COGS**: $3.90

**Packaging Development**
- Designer (Fiverr/99designs): $300
- Box samples: $200
- Card printing setup: $150
- Total: $650 one-time

**D2C Orders**
- **Provider**: ShipStation + ShipBob (3PL)

## Risk Analysis

### Key Risks & Mitigation

**1. Product doesn't resonate emotionally**
- **Risk**: Message falls flat, low conversion
- **Mitigation**: Pre-launch testing with 100 beta customers, A/B test messaging
- **Probability**: Low (universal theme + already validated on Reddit/social)

**2. CAC higher than projected**
- **Risk**: Paid ads cost $15-20 CAC instead of $8-10
- **Mitigation**: Focus on organic (TikTok viral potential), affiliate program, PR
- **Probability**: Medium

**3. Quality issues with manufacturing**
- **Risk**: Engraving quality poor, balls defective
- **Mitigation**: Order samples first, quality inspection before bulk shipping, backup supplier identified
- **Probability**: Low

**4. Copycat products**
- **Risk**: Competitors launch similar motivational balls
- **Mitigation**: Trademark message, build brand fast, create emotional connection with story
- **Probability**: High (but brand loyalty is defensible)

**5. Seasonality**
- **Risk**: Sales drop in slow months (Jan-Mar)
- **Mitigation**: Build email list, nurture sequences, corporate sales focus in Q1
- **Probability**: Medium
